About Me

I am Swayam Kamble currently in my third year of a Bachelor’s in Industrial IoT. I’m passionate about combining technology and problem-solving to develop innovative solutions in electronics and IoT. With projects ranging from smart plugs to security systems, I’ve built a strong foundation in programming, embedded systems, and hands-on hardware development.

Active in my college’s WiSE lab and E-Yantra lab, I also competed in the 2024 eLSI hackathon, earning first place among 520+ colleges. I’m constantly seeking new ways to learn and grow, driven by a vision to contribute meaningfully to IoT and embedded technology.
